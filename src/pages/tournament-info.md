**Novice Eligibility:** Speakers in their first year of competitive university debate, or who have attended less than three tournaments prior to this one will be eligible for novice status. 

## Format

Debates will be in British Parliamentary Format, with speeches seven minutes in length. All motions will be impromptu with fifteen minutes of preparation. There will be five preliminary rounds, with rounds 1, 2, 3 and 5 being open adjudication. 

Motions will be released on Discord and in the GA with no additional walking time between draw and motion drops.

There will be a break to open semifinals and novice finals.

## Ironperson & Opt-Out Policy

We recognise that debate motions, debate rounds, or specific speeches might reference topics that may be triggering in nature. To account for this, any participant (including judges) is welcome to opt-out of a round if they are unwilling or unable to engage with the topic. If this happens, there are two courses of action:

1. The team can choose to iron person
    - In this case, one partner would participate for the team and their ranking and score would be unaffected outside of the absent partner’s speaker score
2. An alternate motion can be provided, in the case that no other resolution is possible.

The equity team also recognises that speakers may participate in a round only to discover arguments and ideas brought up in it present issues to their sense of safety and security and require them to leave. If a speaker leaves after their speech for equity purposes, they will not be penalised.

- If a speaker leaves before their speech their partner can iron person for the round and this will count as one of their opt out rounds.
- Only in the event where both speakers leave before their speeches will they receive a fourth

Participants will be able to opt-out of/iron up to **two rounds** and still maintain break eligible. Please note that during out-rounds, opting-out must happen prior to motion release based on provided content warnings. If a team exceeds using their opt out or ironperson option for more than two rounds, they lose their eligibility to break. 

**To opt-out, please contact a member of the equity team directly.**

## Tech Policy

Participants may use digital notes if they wish (laptops, tablets etc).

- Debaters’ electronic devices (laptops and tablets) MUST be kept on Airplane Mode from motion drop to the end of Opp Whip speech → **NO EXCEPTIONS**
- Use of outside help, the internet, generative AI, and collaborative notetaking software (e.g. shared Google Docs) are not allowed
- Use of smartphones to time speeches is allowed

Should you be found to be in violation of this policy, members of the Equity Team maintain the right to petition your break eligibility. Please remember to be respectful of your fellow debaters and judges!

**If you suspect a violation of the acceptable use policy, please notify a member of the CA team immediately.**
